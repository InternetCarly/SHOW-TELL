What is this?

  • "SHOW & TELL" is a real-time photo sharing website where you choose an image from your camera roll, write a description, and display it in front of a group of people. It's like social media, but like, actually social.

What do I send?

  • Anything! A recent selfie with a story behind it, an art piece you're working on, a picture of an abandoned building, a topical meme, a childhood photo, an image taken moments before disaster, beautiful scenery, a concert pic from your favorite artist, a blurry pic from an insane party, cows on the side of the highway, etc.

Looking for

  • Aesthetically beautiful images
  • Thought-provoking images
  • Bizarre and absurd images
  • Storied images
  • Images that make people laugh

The idea

  • This project was born from my love of "playing camera roll" — the modern-day equivalent of flipping through a photo album and giving a full commentary on every single photo. If there's one thing about me, it's that I will find an excuse to whip out my phone and hold people hostage with my entire camera roll. With "SHOW & TELL", we can all be that guy!
  • Let's play camera roll together!

Troubleshooting

  • Everyone needs to be connected to the same wifi for this to work. Check with the game host to see if you're connected to the right one!
  • If your image is taking forever to appear on the main screen, try refreshing the page. That can help mitigate potential bugs.
